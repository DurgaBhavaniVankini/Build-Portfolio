About Kids Fashion

Kids Fashion (KF) is the online destination for on trend girls’ fashion created by founder and editor Sandi Zaljevic. KF was created out of Sandi's love and passion for kids and fashion, after having two beautiful little girls, Bella (six) and Giselle (three), her interest and talent for styling came to fruition. KF is a creatively curated blog and its complementing social media channels are a beautiful, yet fun and sometimes quirky interpretation of current day children’s fashion, from everyday labels to aspirational luxury brands. Parents far and wide, turn to KF for fashion and lifestyle inspiration and advice for their children, plus to get a glimpse into Sandi's family life in the worlds most liveable city, Melbourne. Sandi's interpretation of fashion and motherhood comes from the heart and is honest and real with no judgement, but to share what she loves. Thank you for visiting KF and feel free to contact Sandi at any time she would love to hear from you.
